About Me

My Background

I'm a front-end developer with a passion for building websites that are both beautiful and usable by everyone. Over the past several years I've worked on projects ranging from small business sites to larger web applications, always with a focus on clean, semantic code and accessibility best practices.

My Approach

I believe good design starts with structure. Before writing a single line of CSS, I think carefully about how a page's content should be organized using proper HTML elements. This makes my sites easier to navigate, faster to load, and more inclusive for people using assistive technology.
